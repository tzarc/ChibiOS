To use this functions you need:

1) #include "stm32_dbg_aid.h" in your chconf.h
2) #define STM32_DBGUART_USE_USART2 TRUE in your mcuconf.h (or any other UART)
3) #define STM32_SOFTRESET_ON_PANIC TRUE in your mcuconf.h (if needed)
4) #define HAL_USE_DBGUART TRUE in your chconf.h
5) #define DBGUART_BITRATE 38400 in your chconf.h
6) properly configure pins of selected uart
